Our Story

Where craft meets eternity

The Forlaloo Philosophy

Forlaloo was born from a simple idea: what if the flowers we give could last as long as our memories?

Our founder, Elara, began creating yarn flowers as gifts for loved ones during difficult times. She wanted to offer something beautiful that wouldn't fadea lasting reminder of love and support.

Today, Forlaloo brings that same intention to every creation. We believe the gifts we give should reflect the permanence of our feelings and the care we have for one another.

Sustainable & Thoughtful Crafting

Every Forlaloo flower begins with high-quality, sustainable materials. We source our yarns from eco-conscious suppliers and use frames designed to last for generations.

Our artisans undergo extensive training to master the techniques that make our flowers special. Each petal is shaped with attention to natural form and movement, creating pieces that feel alive.

We're committed to ethical production practices and fair wages for all our craftspeople, ensuring every flower carries positive energy from creation to delivery.

Caring for Your Forlaloo

Preserve the beauty for generations

Dusting & Cleaning

Gently dust your yarn flowers weekly with a soft brush or a hairdryer on the cool setting. For deeper cleaning, use a vacuum cleaner with a brush attachment on the lowest setting, keeping it at least 6 inches away.

Tip: Avoid using water or liquid cleaners as they may damage the yarn fibers.

Sunlight Exposure

While our yarn is colorfast, prolonged direct sunlight may cause colors to fade over time. Display your Forlaloo flowers in areas with indirect light to preserve their vibrant colors for years.

Tip: Rotate your arrangement periodically if it's near a window for even exposure.

Temperature & Humidity

Yarn flowers are sensitive to extreme temperatures and humidity. Avoid placing them near heating vents, fireplaces, or in damp areas. Ideal conditions are room temperature with moderate humidity.

Tip: In humid climates, consider using a dehumidifier in the display room.
Quick Care Reference
  • Dust weekly with soft brush
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Avoid water and liquid cleaners
  • Store in cool, dry place
  • Handle by stems, not petals
